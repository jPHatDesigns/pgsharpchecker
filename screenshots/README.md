# Screenshots

Place your app screenshots here with these names:
- `main_screen.png` - The main app screen
- `checking.png` - The app while checking for updates
- `notification.png` - The notification when update is available

## How to Take Screenshots

1. Open the app on your Android device
2. Take screenshots using Power + Volume Down
3. Transfer the images to your computer
4. Rename them according to the names above
5. Place them in this `screenshots/` folder

## Recommended Size
- Width: 1080px or similar phone resolution
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Keep file size under 1MB each
